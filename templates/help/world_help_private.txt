Purpose
-------

Creates private project for personal files, photos, finance, and sensitive documents with enhanced security.

Examples
--------

Examples:

  # Personal finance
  dirforge init private --name "finance-2025" --encrypted
  
  # Photo archive
  dirforge init private --name "family-photos"
  
  # Personal documents
  dirforge init private --name "documents" --backup-enabled
